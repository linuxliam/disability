# Getting Started with Disability Advocacy

Welcome to the Disability Advocacy app! This guide will help you get started and make the most of the app's features.

## First Launch

When you first open the app, you'll see:

1. **Home Dashboard** - Your central hub showing an overview of resources, events, and community activity
2. **Navigation** - Use the tab bar (iOS) or sidebar (macOS) to navigate between sections
3. **Search** - Tap the search icon to find resources, events, and community posts

1. **Use Search**: The search function is the fastest way to find what you're looking for

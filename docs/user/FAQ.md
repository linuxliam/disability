# Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the Disability Advocacy app.

## General Questions

### What is the Disability Advocacy app?

The Disability Advocacy app is a comprehensive tool designed to support disability advocacy by providing resources, community connections, events, and advocacy tools in one place.

### Is the app free?

Yes, the app is free to use.

### Does the app work offline?

The app requires an internet connection to load resources and events. Some features may work offline once data is loaded.

### Is my data private?

Yes, your data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect personal information without your consent.

## Features

### How do I search for resources?

Tap the search icon in the navigation bar, then type your search query. You can search across resources, events, and community posts.

### How do I add a resource to favorites?

Tap the heart icon on any resource card or detail view. The resource will be saved to your favorites list.

### Can I add events to my calendar?

Yes! Tap "Add to Calendar" on any event detail page. The event will be added to your system calendar.

### How do I filter resources or events?

Use the category picker at the top of the Resources or Events view to filter by category. You can also use the search bar for more specific filtering.

### Can I share resources or events?

Yes! On macOS, use the share button to share via the native share sheet. On iOS, use the system share sheet.

## Technical Questions

### What platforms does the app support?

The app is available for:
- iOS (iPhone and iPad)
- macOS

### What are the system requirements?

- **iOS**: iOS 15.0 or later
- **macOS**: macOS 15.0 or later

### How do I update the app?

Updates are available through the App Store. The app will notify you when updates are available.

### The app is slow or not loading. What should I do?

1. Check your internet connection
2. Try closing and reopening the app
3. Restart your device
4. Check if there's an app update available

## Accessibility

### Does the app support VoiceOver?

Yes! The app is fully compatible with VoiceOver on both iOS and macOS.

### Can I adjust the text size?

Yes! The app supports Dynamic Type. Adjust text size in your device settings, and the app will adapt.

### Does the app support high contrast mode?

Yes! Enable high contrast mode in the app's Accessibility Settings.

### Can I use the app with keyboard navigation?

Yes! The app supports full keyboard navigation on both platforms.

## Data and Privacy

### Where is my data stored?

Your data (favorites, preferences) is stored locally on your device using UserDefaults.

### Can I export my data?

On macOS, you can export your data through the Data Management section in Settings.

### Can I import data?

On macOS, you can import resources and events from JSON files through the File menu.

### Is my data synced across devices?

Currently, data is stored locally on each device. Cloud sync may be added in future updates.

## Troubleshooting

### The app crashes when I open it

1. Try restarting your device
2. Delete and reinstall the app
3. Check if your device meets the system requirements
4. Contact support if the issue persists

### Search isn't working

1. Check your internet connection
2. Try a different search term
3. Clear the search and try again
4. Restart the app

### Events aren't showing up

1. Check your internet connection
2. Pull down to refresh the events list
3. Try filtering by a different category
4. Restart the app

### I can't add events to my calendar

1. Check that you've granted calendar permissions
2. Go to Settings and enable calendar access
3. Try again
